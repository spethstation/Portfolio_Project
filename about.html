Hi There!


Thanks for stopping by. :)

Front-end development is my most recent career path but I have been a travel agent, casino dealer, maid, executive assistant, HR coordinator, and administrator all at different points in my life. Though varied, all these past careers have shaped me into who I am today and has helped me to build a unique skill set that I utilize in my day-to-day. You might look at my CV and believe I had no idea what I was doing with my life, and you would be correct! However, I feel like I've finally found my calling.

I have over 12+ years experience as a freelance graphic designer and once upon a time I went to university to study fine art, illustration, and art history. I am a very creative individual and am always looking for ways to embed art into my life.

Outside of programming, my passions in life include strength training, soaking up every bit of summer when it comes around, accumulating a giant stack of library books, and playing pinball (badly).
