Hi There!


Thanks for stopping by. :)

My name is Elspeth and I live in beautiful Nova Scotia, Canada.

Front-end development is my most recent career path but I have been a travel agent, casino dealer, maid, executive assistant, HR coordinator, and administrator all at different points in my life. I believe interests and motivations in jobs change like hobbies do over the course of your life. Though varied, all these past careers have shaped me into who I am today and has helped me to build a unique skill set that I utilize in my day-to-day. You might look at my CV and believe I have no idea what I am doing with my life, and you would be correct!

I have over 12+ years experience as an on-again, off-again graphic designer and once upon a time I went to university to study fine art, illustration, and art history. I am a very creative individual and I find ways to be embed it into my every day.

Outside of coding, my passions in life include strength training, driving my little car with music playing, soaking up every bit of summer when it comes around, accumulating a giant stack of library books, playing pinball (badly), and hanging out with my dear family.
